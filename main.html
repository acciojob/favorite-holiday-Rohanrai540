Goa


Goa is a beautiful coastal destination in India known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood.

  1. Baga Beach – Famous for nightlife and water sports
  2. Calangute Beach – Known as the Queen of Beaches
  3. Basilica of Bom Jesus – A historic UNESCO site
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – One of the tallest waterfalls in India
  5. Fort Aguada – A well-preserved Portuguese fort
  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Vindaloo
  • Bebinca
  • Feni
