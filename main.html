Goa


Goa is a popular holiday destination in India known for its beaches, nightlife, history, and delicious food.

  1. Baga Beach – Famous for nightlife and water sports
  2. Calangute Beach – Known as the Queen of Beaches
  3. Basilica of Bom Jesus – A UNESCO World Heritage site
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – Spectacular natural attraction
  5. Fort Aguada – Historic Portuguese fort
  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Chicken Vindaloo
  • Bebinca
  • Feni
